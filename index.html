Hello! 👋

I work on Computer Vision and Machine Learning problems, with a special focus on applications involving 3D Vision, Interactivity and Game Development.

Currently, I am pursuing a Master of Visual Computing degree at Simon Fraser University, and working as a Computer Vision Research intern remotely at the MIx Lab at University of Birmingham under Professor Jianbo Jiao's guidance.

Previously, I have worked as an AI Engineer Intern at VisionBox Inc., focusing on interactive 3D visualization of X-Ray images, and as a Autonomous Vehicles Simulation Software Engineer Intern at Ford Motor Company. I graduated with my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor in 2022.
